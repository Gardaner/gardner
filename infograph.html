Plastageddon

Hectic visualisation to better represent the urgency of the plastic problem in our environment. The problem is not one specific product coming from one specific company or country, so I tried to use a diverse range of comparisons. For this project I took a mainstream approach by literalising data, i.e. 9% of the fish in the Pacific Garbage Patch have plastic in their bodies, so I visualised this as a fish with a body made 9% of plastic. I am happy with how the poster turned out, however I may have made it too busy with the amount of data I wanted to represent. This project would have been better as a series of individual posters, each focusing on one aspect covered in my final infographic.
