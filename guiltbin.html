guiltbin

Environmental awareness campaigns are masters of the art of guilt but they all fall short when it comes to giving a face to the ones causing the problem. That's where Guilt Bin comes in. Guilt Bin is an interactive installation that encourages awareness of the world's waste problem whilst ironically creating more waste. When you toss rubbish into Guilt bin, you get a lovely photo of you, a depressing fact about the waste you just threw away, and you get to add to the problem by doubling your rubbish... you scumbag
