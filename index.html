A bit about me  

Hi I’m Daniel. Have you given any consideration to your car’s extended warranty? This lil website right here is a showcase of my photography and design projects, both academic and personal. I’m an avid photographer, I love a good landscape shot or an off-centre building. When I have spare time, I like to go out and experiment with new photography techniques. You might get to see some of that here, but I’ll mostly just be posting my *best* work because I want you to think I’m perfect. I love being creative and will give almost anything a go, including embroidery. Again, you won’t see that here. Not for free anyway.

My strengths:

Photography, All Adobe applications, User Experience, Reporting, Running quite, fast Cat owner 

My weaknesses:

Not forklift certified, Cat owner 

I am a digital advisor for the Department of Conservation (NZ). In this role I manage the organisation’s intranet, acting as a creator, advisor, and publisher. I have introduced more comprehensive senior management communications reporting that is accessible to a wide range of readers. As the system administrator for our content management system, I provide training for staff as part of the onboarding process and advise on and test improvements. As well as the fine arts, I am passionate about preserving our natural environment and promoting sustainable business. I try to incorporate these values into all the work I do.
